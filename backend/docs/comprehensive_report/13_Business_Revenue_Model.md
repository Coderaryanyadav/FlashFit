# Business Model + Revenue Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Commission on Sales (Primary)
- **Mechanism:** We charge a percentage fee to merchants for every order generated through our platform.
- **Rate:** 15-25% per transaction.

### 2. Delivery Fees
- **Mechanism:** Customers pay a delivery fee based on distance and demand.
- **Rate:** Dynamic (e.g., $2 base + $1/km).
- **Profit:** A portion covers driver payout, the rest is margin.

### 3. Subscription (FlashFit Pro)
- **Mechanism:** Monthly subscription for customers offering free delivery and exclusive discounts.
- **Price:** $9.99/month.
- **Benefit:** Increases Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and retention.

### 4. In-App Advertising
- **Mechanism:** Brands pay for "Sponsored Products" or banner ads within the app.

## Cost Structure
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Marketing spend / New Users.
- **Driver Payouts:** The largest operational cost.
- **Server/Infrastructure:** Cloud costs (Google Cloud).
- **R&D:** Engineering and Design salaries.

## Unit Economics (Example)
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $40
- **Commission (20%):** +$8
- **Delivery Fee:** +$5
- **Driver Payout:** -$4
- **Payment Processing:** -$1
- **Gross Margin:** $8 + $5 - $4 - $1 = **$8 per order**
